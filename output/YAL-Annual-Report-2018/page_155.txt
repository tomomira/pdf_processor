(ii) Interest in joint venture
Middlemount Coal Pty Ltd
A controlled entity, Gloucester (SPV) Pty Ltd, has a 49.9997% interest in the net assets of Middlemount Coal Pty Ltd (“Middlemount”),
an incorporated joint venture, whose principal activity is the development and operation of open-cut coal mines in the Bowen Basin.
HVO entities
On completion of the establishment of the 51%:49% unincorporated joint venture with Glencore on 4 May 2018, the Group holds 51% of
the shares in HVO Coal Sales Pty Ltd, HVO Operations Pty Ltd and HVO Services Pty Ltd (“HVO Entities”). From this date the Group has
determined that it no longer controls these companies. From 4 May 2018 the Group equity accounts the financial results of these companies.
