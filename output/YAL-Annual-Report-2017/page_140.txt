E6 DEED OF CROSS GUARANTEE
Yancoal Australia Ltd and certain subsidiaries (refer to Note E5), are parties to a deed of cross guarantee under which each company guarantees
the debts of the others. By entering into the deed, the wholly owned entities have been relieved from the requirement to prepare a financial
report and Directors’ Report under Legislative Instrument 2016/785 issued by the Australian Securities and Investments Commission.
